### Improved Response

Thank you for reaching out, and I’m sorry for the delay in getting back to you. I understand how frustrating it can be to see a duplicate charge on your account.

I’ll review the billing activity on your subscription and check whether the second charge was processed incorrectly. If the duplicate payment is confirmed, we will either refund the extra charge or explain the next available billing correction step.

I’m escalating this to our billing team for review, and we’ll follow up with an update as soon as possible.

Thank you for your patience.

Best regards,  
Support Team
