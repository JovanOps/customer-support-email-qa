# Improvement Notes

## Common Support Email Issues

### 1. Generic responses

Weak support responses often sound copied and do not address the customer's exact problem.

### 2. Missing empathy

Customers usually need the issue to be acknowledged before receiving instructions.

### 3. No clear next step

A good response should explain what happens next, who is responsible and what the customer can expect.

### 4. Weak escalation handling

Billing, account access and technical failures often require escalation. The customer should be told when escalation is happening.

### 5. Unsupported claims

Support agents should avoid saying that everything is fine unless the issue was clearly checked and explained.

## Recommended QA Approach

A good QA review should identify the issue, score the response, explain the reason behind the score and provide a rewritten version when useful.
